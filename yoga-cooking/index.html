Traditional Indian dishes include a wide range of tastes and offers more healthy food. In addition, traditional Indian cuisine is called for fresh food items with minimum preservatives, compared to typical American fare, it can be very healthy because your body comes in contact with less additive for ingredients. When you think of indian food, you must first have a thing in your mind that indian food is very spicy , Heavy , Hot, Fatty ,greasy. Like many people, you probably will think of something with those lines.this is nothing like this ,it is all wrong. Traditional Indian food includes Fibers,fats.proteins and carbohydrates, which are all elements that are essential for a balanced diet. In Indian cooking, there are spices like turmeric, ginger, garlic and green chilli - all of which are medicinal and healing properties. So, when people think about spicy food, then what do they not realize that the spices used for making food are really good for the body and not just the good taste
